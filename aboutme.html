About me

I love bringing data to life through product insights and machine learning.

As the only Data Scientist at Dropbox, I have worked on the whole data spectrum including robust infrastructure, predictive analytics, and engaging visualizations. Before Dropbox, I interned at Knewton and researched at the Harvard Business School with Professors Ben Edelman and Michael Luca.

I am committed to empowering women in technology and recently spoke at Grace Hopper 2015.

I graduated from Harvard University in 2014 where I earned a joint AB/SM. My Bachelors was in Applied Math in Economics and Computer Science, and my Masters was in Computational Engineering.
