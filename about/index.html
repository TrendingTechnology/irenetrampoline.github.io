About

I work at Dropbox, where I am a data scientist, which covers the range from product insights and analytics to machine learning engineering. Currently I am focusing on document organization. Prior work includes abuse detection, user retention prediction, and company metric definition.

Prior to Dropbox, I studied Applied Math, Computer Science, and Economics at Harvard University where I received a joint AB/SM from the Institute of Computational Science.

In my free time, I enjoy watercolor painting, half-marathon training, and chocolate.
